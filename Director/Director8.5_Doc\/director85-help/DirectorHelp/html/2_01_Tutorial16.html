Pan the camera horizontally

To see the Pan Camera Horizontal behavior in action, do the following:

1

Play the movie and press the Left Arrow key repeatedly.

Each time you press the key, the camera for the 3D world moves a little to the left.

2

Press the Right Arrow key to see the camera change direction.

3

When you finish viewing the panning behaviors, stop and rewind the movie.
