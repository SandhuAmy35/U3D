To learn more

Congratulations! You have learned how to maneuver in a 3D world, including creating and rotating 3D text, using action and trigger behaviors to change the camera perspective, and adding navigation to your 3D world. As you completed the tutorial, you also learned about 3D objects such as lights, models, and cameras, and you learned how actions and triggers rely on groups. To learn more about working with 3D behaviors, see Using 3D behaviors overview.
