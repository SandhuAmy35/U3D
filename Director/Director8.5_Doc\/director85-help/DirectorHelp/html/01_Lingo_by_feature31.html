Operators

These terms are operators available in Lingo.

 
Math operators

Use these terms for math statements:

* (multiplication)

<> (not equal)

/ (division)

> (greater than)

+ (addition)

>= (greater than or equal to)

- (minus)

< (less than)

= (equals)

<= (less than or equal to)

 
Comparison operators

Use and, not, and or to compare expressions.
