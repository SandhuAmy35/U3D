Enabling 1000 connections to the Multiuser Server

The Multiuser Server, by default, allows 50 users to connect. You can enable 1000 simultaneous connections to the server by modifying the multiuser.cfg file.

To enable 1000 connections to the server:

1

In the Multiuser Server 2.1 subfolder of your Director 8 application folder, locate the Multiuser.cfg file and open it as a text file.

2

In the Director users licensing information section, find the line that asks for the ServerOwnerName. Delete the pound sign at the beginning of the line and replace the Enter Your Name Here text with your name.

3

On the next line, delete the pound sign and replace the serial number with a valid serial number, and then save and close the file.
