View the completed movie

You can view a completed version of the tutorial movie to become familiar with how your finished movie will appear.

1

Launch Director, and then choose File > Open.

2

Browse to the Director 8.5 application folder and open Learning/8.5_Tutorial/Magic_finished.dir.

3

To play the movie, click the Play button on the Control Panel or choose Control > Play.

Notice that a subtle light appears to shine on the text as it rotates.

4

To get a sense of the behaviors that you'll apply in the tutorial, do the following:

With the pointer, click the magic objects on the tables

Press the arrow keys

Press the F and B keys to move forward and backward within the scene

Press the Spacebar

5

When you finish viewing the movie, either click Stop on the Control Panel or choose Control > Stop.
