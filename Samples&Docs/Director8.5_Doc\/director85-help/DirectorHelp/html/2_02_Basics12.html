Groups

Groups are collections of models, lights, cameras, or other groups. Groups allow you to rotate or translate their contents simultaneously. A group has a name, a transform, and a parent, and it can have one or more children. It has no other properties. The highest level group is the group called "world," which is essentially synonymous with the 3D cast member itself. For more information, see Groups.
