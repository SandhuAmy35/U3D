Slow the rotation speed

To slow the rotation speed setting, complete the following steps:

1

With the Title text sprite selected, click the Property Inspector's Behavior tab.

2

With Automatic Model Rotation (Internal) selected in the Property Inspector, click the down arrow to the right of Rotation Speed and move the Rotation Speed slider from 10 to 3.

3

Play the movie to view the text rotating at a slower speed. When you finish, stop and rewind the movie.

Note: To return to the parameters dialog box for any behavior, you can double-click the behavior on the Property Inspector's Behavior tab.
